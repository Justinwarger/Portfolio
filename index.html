The Process

First Meeting

This is our first time talking to each other! It can be through any form that works for you. My main goal is to figure out what the goal of your website is. To showcase your work, or make it easy for people to see what you are all about.

Site Structure & Layout

After I have an idea of what goal you need your website to accomplish. I can start formulating a layout and structure that will function efficiently for your users. This is where you will start to see where information will be on the page.

Working Prototype

Now that I have a plan in place and structure established I will start developing a rough design layout. This is where you will be able to see how the content moves and looks on all devices.

Details

The information is on the page and the color scheme and images are working together. Now I will add all the details. These include links, hover states, navigation, and many other things required for an amazing website.

You're in Control

I hand over the keys to you. I use Wordpress to give you the control panel to add and change anything you want on your site. You do not have to know how to code!

Deployment

Your site is officially launched! The process does not end there. I will always be available for support and design consulting. Time to celebrate and let the world see your website!

A Custom Responsive Design

I specialize in creating an experience for your users that is tailored to your needs. A completely custom design that looks fantastic on all devices. Together we can maximize the potential of your online presence. The value of a website can be easily overlooked. Don't make the mistake of undervaluing the potential of a quality web page.
